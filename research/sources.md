# Research Sources — Cold Outreach Pipeline for B2B SaaS

## Why I Chose This Topic
Cold outreach is the most direct and measurable growth channel 
for B2B SaaS companies. Understanding how top practitioners 
build, run, and optimize cold outreach pipelines provides the 
strongest foundation for a real, actionable playbook.

## Expert Selection Methodology
Experts were selected based on three criteria:
1. Verified results with specific numbers (not claims)
2. Active content creation in 2024–2026 (not outdated advice)
3. Unique angle not covered by other experts on this list
